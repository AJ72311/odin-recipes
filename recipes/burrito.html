Breakfast Burrito Recipe

Description

A warm flour tortilla packed with fluffy scrambled eggs, crispy bacon, melted cheese, and golden hash browns—this breakfast burrito is a hearty, grab-and-go meal that brings together classic morning flavors in every bite. Customizable and satisfying, it’s the perfect way to start the day whether you’re at home or on the move.

Ingredients

  • 2 large flour tortillas (10-12 inch size)
  • 4 large eggs
  • 4 strips of bacon
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup frozen hash browns or diced potatos (cooked)
  • 2 tbsp milk or cream (for fluffier eggs)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbsp butter or oil (for cooking)

Steps

  1. Cook the bacon
  2. Cook the hash browns
  3. Scramble the eggs
  4. Warm the tortillas
  5. Asssemble the burritos
  6. Fold and roll
  7. Toast it
  8. Serve
