Cheesy Chicken Quesadilla Recipe

Description

Golden, crisp flour tortillas encase a melty, savory filling of seasoned chicken, gooey cheese, and saut√©ed peppers. This quesadilla is pan-grilled to perfection, with a satisfying crunch in every bite and a cheesy pull that makes it irresistibly good. Perfect as a snack, appetizer, or full meal when served with salsa, sour cream, or guacamole.

Ingredients

  • 2 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup cooked, shredded chicken
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/2 cup thinly-sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup sliced onions
  • 1 tbsp olive oil or butter
  • Salt, pepper, and a pinch of cumin
  • Salsa, sour cream, or guacamole (for serving)

Steps

  1. Prep the filling
  2. Assemble the quesadilla
  3. Cook it
  4. Slice and serve
