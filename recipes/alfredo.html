Shrimp Fettuccine Alfredo Recipe

Description

Golden, sautéed shrimp—seasoned with garlic, salt, and a touch of pepper—nestle atop a bed of perfectly cooked fettuccine noodles. The pasta is generously coated in a velvety Alfredo sauce made from real butter, heavy cream, and freshly grated Parmesan cheese, creating a luxurious, smooth texture with a savory depth. Flecks of parsley and cracked black pepper add color and contrast, while a sprinkle of extra cheese melts delicately over the top. The dish is warm, indulgent, and aromatic, with each bite offering the briny sweetness of shrimp balanced by the richness of the sauce and the heartiness of the pasta.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 1 lb shrimp (peeled and deveined)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil or butter
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper (to tase)
  • Fresh parsley (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Cook the shrimp
  3. Make the alfredo sauce
  4. Add parmesan cheese
  5. Combine everything
  6. Serve
